I am a postdoc in the Theory Group at EPFL hosted by Ola Svensson. I obtained my Ph.D. at Queen Mary University of London under the supervision of Justin Ward.

I work in combinatorial optimisation, and approximation algorithms. I am interested in these topics and more broadly in theoretical computer sciences. I am found of maximization problems over independence systems. This includes among others: k-Set Packing, Submodular Function Maximization, Matroid Intersection, and Matching.

I am always interested in bridging theory and practice, and happy to discuss research. Feel free to reach out!

When I am not doing research you can find me playing Ultimate Frisbee with FlyHigh Lausanne.
